Welcome to the Digital Exploration of Bendung Pataruman

Bendung Pataruman is an important hydraulic infrastructure in West Java, Indonesia, built to regulate irrigation, manage water resources, and mitigate floods in the surrounding regions. This web application allows users to interactively explore Pataruman in 3D, visualize terrain data, and understand its structure using modern geospatial technology.

About Bendung Pataruman

Bendung Pataruman is located in Ciamis Regency, West Java, serving as a critical structure for irrigation and water distribution from the Citanduy River. It was developed to optimize agricultural productivity and ensure consistent water supply throughout the year. With advances in spatial technology, the 3D mapping of this infrastructure helps in better planning, maintenance, and educational dissemination.

The digital twin representation of Bendung Pataruman offers real-time visualization of terrain elevation, construction modeling, and spatial analytics, supporting better public understanding and expert analysis.
